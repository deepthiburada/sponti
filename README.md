# Sponti

> _You be you. Meet through the activity._

Sponti is an activity-first meetup app that lets you see who's
going hiking, climbing, running, or to the farmers market near
you right now — and join them in one tap. No dating profiles,
no event planning. Just real people showing up to the same place
because they both wanted to go anyway.

---

## The founding story

I grew up in a remote village in India. Moving to the US was the
biggest thing I had ever done — and for a long time, the loneliest.
Every time I talked to a stranger on a trail or at a climbing spot,
I came home feeling human again. That experience didn't just inspire
the idea. It is the idea.
